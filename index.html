About Jephthah

Excellence, Discipline, Christian Values — nearly three decades of shaping futures in Port Harcourt.

Jephthah Comprehensive Secondary School was established on October 6, 1995 with a vision to provide world-class education rooted in Christian values. The name "Jephthah" means "God Opens" — reflecting our belief that education opens every door.

Over nearly three decades, we have grown from a small initiative to one of the leading institutions in Port Harcourt, known for academic excellence, discipline, and character development. We serve over 1,200 students across Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels in both boarding and day programmes.

Vision: "Train up a child in the way he should go…" — Proverbs 22:6

Mission: To provide comprehensive Christian education that promotes excellence, self-discipline, and motivation — producing disciples of Christ who advance the global community.
