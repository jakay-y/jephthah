About Jephthah

Established 1995 | Excellence, Discipline, Christian Values

Our History

Jephthah Comprehensive Secondary School was established on October 6, 1995, as a vision to provide world-class education rooted in Christian values. The name "Jephthah" means "God Opens," reflecting our belief that education opens doors for our students.

Over nearly three decades, we have grown from a small initiative to a leading educational institution in Port Harcourt, known for academic excellence, discipline, and character development. We serve over 1,200 students across nursery, primary, and secondary levels, in both boarding and day programs.

Vision & Mission

Vision: "Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old, he will not depart from it" - Proverbs 22:6

Mission: To provide comprehensive Christian education in a supportive environment that promotes excellence in learning, self-discipline, and motivation, ensuring that students become disciples of Jesus Christ and advance the global community.

Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact us or download the prospectus to learn more about our programs.

Step 2: Application

Submit online form with required documents (transcripts, passport, medical forms).

Step 3: Assessment

Take entrance examination to assess academic level and placement.

Step 4: Interview

Parent and student interview to ensure cultural fit and discuss expectations.

Step 5: Admission Letter

Receive acceptance and payment of registration deposit to secure placement.
